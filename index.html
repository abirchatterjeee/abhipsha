We Were Never Just Friends

A Journey from Almost Strangers to Almost Everything

They weren’t meant to fall in love. But they did—slowly, unexpectedly, and deeply.

But life doesn’t wait for feelings to settle. When love was tested by real-world struggles, heartbreak, and distance, their bond was forced to face the hardest question of all.

Can something that was never supposed to be... still survive everything?
